                        A civilian whose daily routine revolves around the comforts of home, office work, and social engagements. His
                        lifestyle is characterized by a predictable rhythm of work,leisure, and personal pursuits. On the flip side,a
                        dedicated soldier whose life is molded by discipline, duty, and a commitment to service. His days are  marked
                        by rigorous training, mission readiness, and the unique challenges that come with a military lifestyle.In the 
                        midst of these distinct worlds, the concept of the National Defence Academy (NDA) emerges as a bridge between
                        civilian life and the military sphere. I too wanted to live and experience a lifestyle a soldier lives and how
                        the spirit of forgiving everything for the sake of his nation arises. The NDA becomes a symbol of the transfor
                        mative journey ,transitioning from civilian life to the disciplined and duty-bound existence of a military prof
                        essional.he journey began with the National Defence Academy (NDA) exam—an intellectual and character crucible
                        that tested not just knowledge, but resilience and dedication.Upon successfully surmounting the NDA hurdle, the
                        path led to the rigorous 5-day Service Selection Board (SSB) training—a transformative experience that delved beyond the academic realm.
